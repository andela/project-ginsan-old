Cards For Humanity


Unlike most of the party games you've played before, Cards For Humanity is as despicable and awkward as you and your friends. The game is simple. Each round, one player asks a question from a black card, and everyone else answers with their funniest white card.

Learn more »

How to Play

Step one

Each player begins with, and will always have, 10 white answer cards.

Step two

For each round, one player is randomly chosen as the Card Czar.

Step three

Everyone else answers the black question card by clicking on the answer card they want to use.

Step four

The Card Czar then picks a favorite answer, and whoever played that answer wins the round.
